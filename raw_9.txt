### Simplifying Heckler for a Mobile-First Comedy Club Experience

To simplify Heckler and focus on a mobile-first comedy club experience, we can prioritize the following core user flows and features:

**Core User Flows:**

1. **Onboarding**: Users download the app, create an account, and are introduced to the comedy club experience.
2. **Comedian Discovery**: Users browse and discover new comedians, with options to filter by style, topic, or popularity.
3. **Comedy Show**: Users attend a live comedy show, with the ability to interact with the comedian and other audience members through live chat and reactions.
4. **Content Library**: Users can access a library of pre-recorded comedy content, including audio clips, videos, and written jokes.

**Core Features:**

1. **Comedian Durable Objects**: Utilize Comedian Durable Objects to generate high-quality, engaging comedy content.
2. **TTS Audio Streaming**: Leverage TTS audio streaming to provide an immersive audio experience for users.
3. **Caching**: Implement caching to ensure fast and seamless content delivery.
4. **Live Chat and Reactions**: Include live chat and reaction features to enable user interaction and engagement during comedy shows.
5. **Personalization**: Offer personalized recommendations for comedians and content based on user preferences and behavior.

**Features to Remove or Deprioritize:**

1. **Complex Navigation**: Simplify the app's navigation to focus on the core user flows and features.
2. **Non-Essential Features**: Remove or deprioritize features that are not essential to the comedy club experience, such as excessive settings or unnecessary social sharing options.
3. **Desktop Support**: Focus on mobile-first development, with desktop support as a secondary consideration.

**New Features to Consider:**

1. **Comedian Scheduling**: Allow users to schedule and attend live comedy shows with their favorite comedians.
2. **User-Generated Content**: Enable users to create and share their own comedy content, such as jokes or funny stories.
3. **Social Sharing**: Implement social sharing features that allow users to share their favorite comedy content with friends and family.
4. **Rewards and Incentives**: Develop a rewards system that incentivizes users to engage with the app, attend live shows, and share content with others.

**Technical Considerations:**

1. **Cloudflare Workers**: Utilize Cloudflare Workers to handle caching, content delivery, and other technical aspects of the app.
2. **Durable Objects**: Leverage Durable Objects to manage comedian data, scheduling, and other critical components of the app.
3. **TTS Engine**: Integrate a high-quality TTS engine to provide an immersive audio experience for users.

By focusing on these core user flows and features, we can create a simplified and engaging mobile-first comedy club experience that showcases the best of Heckler's Comedian Durable Objects, TTS audio streaming, and caching capabilities.
